Support & sponsorship

Umbraculum is community-first in what gets built and honest about capacity (architecture, maintainer time, AI compute). This page states what we ask for and what sponsorship does not buy.

Collaborate (highest leverage)

Code, reviews, documentation, tests, accessibility work, execution plans, and contributions to the open-source umbraculum-toolset Cursor plugin pack all compound for every future contributor.

Start with Getting started and CONTRIBUTING.md.

Sponsorship (optional)

We welcome help carrying AI compute / tokens, maintainer time, and infrastructure so the project can ship more of what the community has already voted on — not what a sponsor privately prefers.

Sponsorship does not buy:

  • Vote weight or queue priority
  • Veto immunity or private decision channels
  • Feature paywalls — AGPLv3 core stays available to everyone on the same terms

Sponsorship can buy: public acknowledgement (with opt-out), the same visibility into in-flight work as any other community member, and the satisfaction of helping the project stay sustainable under MANIFESTO §2.1.

Donation channels are not wired yet (GitHub Sponsors, Open Collective, or equivalent will be linked here after the July 2026 public-alpha discussion). The principles above are the commitment; the mechanism is operational detail landing at flip.
