Umbraculum

A toolset for building workspace-shaped operational applications — from product information and quality workflows to manufacturing planning, inventory, and automation. One platform orchestrator, one AI consultant, modules you compose.

Students & university labs

Umbraculum is free to clone, self-host, and experiment with — capstones, coursework prototypes, hackathons, and portfolio verticals. Public alpha is aimed at learning the platform shape, not replacing production ERP on day one.

Is this for you?

  • Good fit

    Build or extend a workspace app (web, optional native). Self-host on a laptop or lab machine. Learn TypeScript, composable modules, and contracts — or ship a Tier 6 vertical in your own repo.

  • Look elsewhere

    You need accounting, payroll, or full-suite ERP on a fixed go-live date, vendor SLA, or mature warehouse apps today — e.g. Odoo Community or your institution's existing stack.

What you get

  • Horizontal platform

    Auth, workspaces, billing hooks, rendering, i18n, and cross-platform UI primitives.

  • Canonical modules

    Peer domains — MRP, CRP, PIM, automation, and more — not a single vertical silo.

  • AI consultant

    Module tools, workspace memory, reporting queries, and product-doc retrieval — BYOK.

  • Web + native

    One source of truth ships to browser and device; brewery is the stress-test showcase.

Public alpha

We are preparing a July 2026 public alpha: source visibility, contributor docs, Cursor toolset plugins, and MIT SDK packages on npm. The hosted product is self-build today — clone, compose, run.
